🍷 WOW vs. Douro Valley day trip

Decided, but kept for reference on why.

✓ Decided: WOW, Vila Nova de Gaia — a Douro day trip would eat your only full free day and land you back after dark.
AspectWOW World of WineDouro Valley day trip
Time costHalf day (2–4 hrs)7–10 hrs — your entire Jan 6 or 7
Getting there10–15 min walk over Dom Luís I Bridge1–1.5 hrs each way by van on winding roads
Effort for parentsLow — indoor, seated tasting, own paceModerate–high — long day, uneven ground at quintas
January daylightIrrelevant — mostly indoorsReturns after dark; valley views lost late in the day
Weather riskLowHigh — outdoor viewpoints and river cruise
Cost pp~€30 all-day pass~€140–150 incl. lunch & cruise
On a 4-day trip where two days are half-days, a Douro trip costs you 25% of your usable time. WOW gives you the Port-wine experience, a proper lunch with a view, and you're back across the bridge in 15 minutes.
